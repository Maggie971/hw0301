Glaucoma Awareness

What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is an eye disease causing vision loss due to optic nerve damage, often from high eye pressure.

Identifying Glaucoma

Early-stage glaucoma often lacks symptoms, making regular eye exams key for detection. Signs can include peripheral vision loss, tunnel vision, blurred vision, or seeing halos.
